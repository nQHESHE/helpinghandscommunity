Who We Are

Helping Hands Community is a non-profit organisation focused on uplifting communities through support, care, and development programmes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to reduce hardship by providing food support, education assistance, and community outreach programmes.

Our Vision

We envision a world where all communities are strong, united, and self-sufficient.

Our Core Values

  • Compassion
  • Respect
  • Unity
  • Integrity
  • Service

What We Do

Helping Hands Community is committed to creating positive and lasting change in communities through meaningful support and development initiatives.

Our Goals

  • Improve the quality of life for vulnerable individuals and families.
  • Promote community development and social inclusion.
  • Encourage volunteerism and community participation.
  • Provide sustainable support programmes that empower communities.

Our Objectives

  • Provide food and basic necessities to those in need.
  • Support educational programmes and learning opportunities.
  • Organise community outreach and awareness campaigns.
  • Partner with local organisations to maximise community impact.
  • Promote environmental responsibility through clean-up initiatives.

  • Food relief programmes
  • Education support
  • Environmental clean-ups
  • Awareness campaigns
