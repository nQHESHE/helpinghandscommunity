Welcome to Helping Hands Community

We are dedicated to supporting communities through education , food relief , environment initiatives and volunteer programmes.

We are a non-profit organisation committed to building stronger communities through kindness, support, and sustainable development.

Who We Are

Helping Hands Community is built on the belief that small actions can create big change. We focus on providing support to communities in need through food relief, education support, and awareness programmes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support and uplift underprivileged individuals and families by providing essential resources , promoting education and encouraging community involvement through acts of care and compassion.

Our Impact

What We Do

  • Food support programmes for struggling families
  • Community awareness and education campaigns
  • Donation drives for essential supplies
  • Environmental clean-up projects

Why Support Us?

Every donation, volunteer hour, and act of kindness helps us reach more people in need. Your support directly impacts lives and strengthens communities.
