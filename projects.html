Project Directory

🥫 Food Relief Programme

We provide essential food parcels to families facing food insecurity. This programme ensures that households have access to nutritious meals during difficult times.

📚 Education Support Programme

We support learners with school supplies, learning materials, and tutoring assistance to help improve academic performance and reduce dropout rates.

🌱 Community Clean-Up Campaign

We organise clean-up initiatives to promote cleaner, healthier environments in local communities. This helps reduce pollution and encourages environmental responsibility.

❤️ Awareness & Outreach Programme

We run awareness campaigns to educate communities about health, wellbeing, and available support services.

🏘️ Community Development Projects

These projects focus on long-term improvement such as skills development, empowerment programmes, and building stronger communities.
